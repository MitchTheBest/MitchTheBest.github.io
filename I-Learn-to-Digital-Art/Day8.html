I wanted to do an interior for this painting but I ran out of time so I wasn't able to get to the detailing phase which is the reason I wanted to do the interior in the first place. The plan was to make a wizards study that was cluttered so that I would have to detail everything out so it could be understandable. I also wanted to attempt to use a more interesting lighting method were the light source was in frame (the chandelier) and a specific color. I plan on coming back to this painting in the future because I really like the design and ideas I had for it but they were never realized so I'm upset. The perspective gave me a few issues because as you can see the line art doesn't align with the color because the original line art was off in some places and I had to fix it many different times because I didn't notice for a while.

Likes

  1. The design and my future plans
  2. I got myself to draw actual perspective lines even though they were poor and that it's only 1 point perspective

Dislikes

  1. There are perspective errors still
  2. The line art is sort of useless because its perspective wasn't even right and doesn't have all the details I was planning on adding later
  3. The large book's bottom feels weird in perspective even though I tried to fix it multiple times
  4. I feel as though I am spending way too much time on the little things like keeping a line straight instead of focusing on the big picture

I'm not quite sure what I'm going to do for my next painting but I'm thinking about doing some sort of study so I see some improvement in a specific area but I don't know what and I don't want it to be long and boring. I've been only doing fun stuff thus far because I want to be able to stay motivated to keep working at it and improving because I know it's easiest to quit when you aren't seeing any improvement and not having fun.
