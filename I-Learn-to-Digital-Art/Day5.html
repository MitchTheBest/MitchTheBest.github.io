This painting is sort of a piece of fan art for the game dark souls because it is heavily based off of a character in Dark Souls 1 but not exactly the same. It took me around 3 and a half hours to bring to this state which is good because I actually was able to spend at least a decent amount of time on it unlike last painting. This painting has a lot of the same issues as painting 3, I'm still having an issue with the shading and lack of perspective causing the image to look flat. I think part of that is because I didn't finish my shading because I was getting frustrated and annoyed. My plan was to focus on the details of the shield, shoulder, and arm brace but the way that I was attempting to didn't feel like it was working at all so I got very hasty and made the shield look horrible compared to the rest of the painting which is disappointing because I didn't try.

Likes

  1. I like the light and shade of where I actually did it to a complete state
  2. The mask turned out as good as I wanted and hoped that it would

Dislikes

  1. I started to get frustrated and it is clearly visible where I was working on in those moments
  2. I didn't spend enough time planning and with line work
  3. I quit with the painting prematurely
  4. All of my paintings so far have the too clean digital look and they are not executed good enough for that to look good yet

I plan on doing some sort of environment painting next where I only use one layer and don't start with very much line art so I can try to be more fluid and have a painterly looking outcome.
