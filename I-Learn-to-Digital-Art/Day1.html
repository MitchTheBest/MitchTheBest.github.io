Day 1

So this is the first painting of this endeavour, overall I am pretty happy with the result, as it is better than anything I expected that i would beable to create at this time. I spent around 2 to 3 hours which I realize that I could've spent much more time on it but at that point I felt as though I didn't know how to proceed with anything and I was getting frustrated with my lack of skill and knowledge so I thought it would be best if I just stopped so that I would feel like continuing and learning tomorrow. I plan on watching some quick tutorial videos to see if I can rectify some technique errors early. Now I'm gonna go through the painting and describe what I like and dislike about the piece.

Likes

  1. The overall quality, I feel like for a first painting, it's a great start
  2. The complexity of the image and form seem pretty good for a start

Dislikes

  1. The shading and highlights, I did not know how to properly or easily blend so the way that I was trying was painful and slow with a poor result
  2. The perspective is a bit off, you can easily tell by comparing the two engines, the one that's suppose to be further is much longer and some other issues
  3. The overall value of the painting, it doesnt read well because I didn't think about that before starting
  4. I decided to stop when I knew that I could of continued and tried to add more and refine further but I didn't because I was getting annoyed
  5. And then a bunch of little stuff

Overall, I had a good time with this painting and hope that I can keep to it and see my improvement over the weeks to come. I now realize that complications may arrive with doing a painting everyday since I am also a fulltime student and seeing as this one painting took me 3 hours and it is very minimal, I might have to limit the time I spend on each painting or just not do it everyday, which will mean that I will improve slower but thats fine for a hobbyist anyways, as long as I'm doing something.
