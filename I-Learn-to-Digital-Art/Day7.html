I am very proud of this painting because I can see the improvement and I'm spending more time on them now so they look more finished and I'm very motivated. I spent around 8 hours on this one, using the same technique as before where I only use one layer and have little to no line drawing. There are many parts of it that I don't like because I was having trouble with the perspective of the face for a long time during the painting process and I kept changing it over and over and wasn't happy so different parts have differing levels of completion and it doesn't really come together as a whole. Also, midway through, I discovered a new way to shade and have more detail so I was experimenting with it in different parts and I think it doesn't look unified because of that.

Likes

  1. The color choices even though they look off
  2. The fur neck scarf is nice but is much worse than before because I had to redo it and i didn't put enough effort in the second time

Dislikes

  1. The colors look to desaturated and muddy
  2. I didn't try and stick with painting the eyes because I couldn't get them to work in the first few minutes
  3. The face and skin has no real shading so looks strange and flat next to everything else
  4. The horns are too smooth and the shading isn't very accurate
  5. The hair doesn't look right, it's supposed to be clean and curled but it doesn't even look like hair

For my next painting I would like to do an interior so that I have to focus on perspective heavily if I want it to look good at all. I don't know if I want to continue using the style of no layers and little line drawing because it seems slow and overly difficult because I kept having to paint the same areas over again.
